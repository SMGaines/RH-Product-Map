Red Hat Ansible Automation Platform
Red Hat Ansible Automation Platform automates IT tasks such as provisioning,installing, configuring and updating systems, devices, software and applications, across the Enterprise & Cloud, in order to improve productivity, lower costs, & reduce risk
Red Hat Satellite (Smart Management)

Red Hat Insights
Red Hat Insights is a cloud-based service that is included as part of a RHEL subscription that allows customers to automatically maintain the health of their RHEL systems with respect to security, reliability and performance
