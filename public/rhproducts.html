Red Hat Ansible Automation Platform
Red Hat Ansible Automation Platform automates IT tasks such as provisioning,installing, configuring and updating systems, devices, software and applications, across the Enterprise & Cloud, in order to improve productivity, lower costs, & reduce risk
Red Hat Satellite (Smart Management)
Red Hat Satellite is Red Hat's management solution that allows customers to provision, configure, administrate and manage subscriptions for their entire RHEL estate
Red Hat Insights
Red Hat Insights is a cloud-based service, included as part of both RHEL and OpenShift subscriptions, that allows customers to automatically maintain the health of their RHEL/OpenShift systems with respect to security, reliability and performance
Red Hat Integration
Red Hat Integration consists of 4 components that together enable customers to connect systems, applications, information and people together
Red Hat Fuse Connects applications & systems together
Red Hat 3Scale API Management for managing & securing APIs
Red Hat A-MQ Message queuing technology to allow applications to exchange information
Red Hat A-MQ Streams Provides the ability to aggregate huge numbers of events from disparate systems so they can be processed for business insight/intelligence
Red Hat Process Automation
Red Hat Process Automation consists of 3 components that enable customers to automate business processes & decisions, and solve optimisation challenges
Red Hat Process Automation Manager Enables Business Analysts to design,improve & automate business processes
Red Hat Decision Manager Enables Business Analysts to dynamically change business application behaviour without the need for application code changes
Red Hat Business Resource Planner Enables the business to solve complex resource optimisation challenges such as Employee Rostering or Vehicle Routing
Red Hat OpenShift Container Platform
Red Hat OpenShift Container Platform is an application platform, based on Containers, Kubernetes & RHEL, that allows customers to build and/or migrate any type of business workload and run them securely, reliably and scalably, anywhere across the Open Hybrid Cloud (Physical, Virtual, Private Cloud, Public Cloud, Edge)
Red Hat Advanced Cluster Management for Kubernetes
Red Hat Advanced Cluster Management for Kubernetes enables Administrators to provision and manage distributed OpenShift installations enabling these 'islands' to be viewed as one large, global, centrally managed OpenShift installation. In a phrase, 'Open Hybrid Cloud Management'
Red Hat OpenShift Container Storage
Red Hat OpenShift Container Storage is an add-on subscription to OpenShift Container platform that provides storage for business applications, using a common interface, anywhere across the Open Hybrid Cloud
Red Hat CodeReady
Red Hat CodeReady Workspaces provides developers with a consistent, secure, preconfigured development environment in their browser, delivered by & connected to OpenShift, that speeds cloud-native development.
Red Hat Runtimes
Red Hat Runtimes provides a wide range of OpenShift-based frameworks & tools for developing, or migrating to, cloud-native applications. It therefore enables developers to choose the right tool for the right job and thereby effectively address any development challenge, now or in the future
Red Hat Enterprise Linux

Edge Computing
Edge Computing refers to computing resources being placed close to the user/customer/device at the 'edge' of the network in order to provide faster and more reliable experiences for customers and users
